# Hi I'm Durga Anand 

### Year 3 CS AI Student @ Heriot-Watt University Dubai

Hey there! I'm Durga, a Computer Science (AI) undergraduate at Heriot-Watt University Dubai, and someone who took the road less travelled to get here. I came from a Biology background in school and chose Computer Science simply because I wanted to try something completely new and honestly? Best decision I ever made.

I'm still growing as a developer, but what I lack in years of experience I make up for in curiosity, dedication, and a genuine love for what I'm learning. During my time at Heriot-Watt I've gotten hands-on with a wide range of languages and technologies. Whether it's building database-driven web apps, exploring AI and machine learning, or diving into UI/UX design, I give everything my full attention. I'm the kind of person who doesn't move on until the job is done, and done well.

I've also had the chance to take my learning beyond the classroom. In July 2025 I completed a Digital Services internship at Khatib & Alami – Consolidated Engineering Company, where I explored geospatial AI, location intelligence, and digital twins. I worked hands-on with Python and open-source datasets applying regression models, dived into Esri's deep learning tools, and studied super resolution and its real-world machine learning applications. It was a brilliant first taste of AI in industry.

Beyond the technical side, I'm passionate about people and community. In my first year at university I served as Marketing & Media Executive for Google Developer Group on Campus at Heriot-Watt, running social media campaigns and promoting AI and tech events. In my second year I stepped into an Event Support Coordinator role for the same club. I also volunteer at every opportunity I get, from cybersecurity hackathons to panel discussions featuring speakers from Google, Oracle, and more. If there's an event, chances are I'm there helping make it happen.

I'm currently looking for internships and entry-level opportunities in AI and tech where I can learn, contribute, and grow.

Outside of tech, I'm fluent in English and Malayalam, and the owner of a very niche but very beloved collection of 120+ KitKat wrappers from flavours across the world 🍫 — six years in the making and still going strong.

- 🌍 Based in Dubai, UAE
- ✉️ [Durga.anand311@gmail.com](mailto:Durga.anand311@gmail.com)
